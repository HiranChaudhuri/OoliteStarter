OXPs/OXZ Status column

The OXPs/OXZs tab's table on the left has these columns:

  • Identifier - the internal identifier for Oolite
  • Date - the release date
  • Title - the name of the expansion alotted by it's author
  • Version - the version of the expansion
  • Category - categories this expansion plays in
  • Tags - some more or less useful tags
  • Status - a quite comprehensive status display showing five flags in little space. You need to decode if there is a capital or small letter:

    O
    o    		The expansion is online and can be downloaded
    The expansion is not online
    L
    l    		The expansion is locally available
    The expansion is not locally available
    E
    e    		The expansion is enabled
    The expansion is not enabled
    R
    r    		The expansion requires other expansions
    The expansion does not require other expansions
    C
    c    		The expansion declares to be conflicting with other expansions
    The expansion does not declare any conflicts

OXP/OXZ Tag Colors

The OXPs/OXZs tab's detail pane on the right contains colorful tags in the bottm:

■ Pay attention to items with this color. They may explain or require fixing.

■ Ready to download and install.

■ Installed and at the current version.

■ Installed; updated pack available for download.

■ Ready to download, but Oolite will also download extra packs to make it work.

■ Conflicts with an already installed pack.

■ Incompatible with your selected version of Oolite.

■ Manually installed: cannot also be installed automatically (eg. a tweaked oxp in the AddOns folder)

■ Installed but no longer available for download.

■ Worthwhile knowing.

The color scheme is based on the in-game expansions manager.
